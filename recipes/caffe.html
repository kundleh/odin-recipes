Caffè

Description

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain flowering plants in the Coffea genus.

The coffee is darkly colored, slightly acidic and has a stimulating effect in humans, primarily due its caffeine content.

Ingredients:

  • Moka
  • Coffee powder
  • Water
  • A cup
  1. Take the moka and open it.
  2. Put the water, from a bottle or from your sink, and pour it in the moka kettle.
  3. Put the coffee powder in the funnel filter and then close the moka.
  4. Place the moka on the stove and wait till the coffee comes out.
  5. Pour the coffee in your cup.
